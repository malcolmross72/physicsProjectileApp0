Projectiles

In the 1600s, armies used equations of motion to calculate velocities and angles for firing missiles to hit targets.

Abbreviations

  • meters, m
  • kilometers, km
  • meters per second, m/s
  • meters per second squared, m/s2

Notations

AbbreviationMeaning
uInitial velocity
vFinal velocity
aAcceleration
tTime
sDistance

Equations of Motion

  1. v = u + a * t
  2. s = u * t + 0.5 * a * t2
