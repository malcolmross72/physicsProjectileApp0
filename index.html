Projectiles

In the 1600s, armies used equations of motion to calculate velocities and angles for firing missiles to hit targets. While a quarterback does not do explicit calculations using equations of motion, a computerized football game will certainly need to do these calculations.

Abbreviations

  • meters, m
  • kilometers, km: 1000 meters make up a kilometer
  • meters per second, m/s: units for measuring distance
  • meters per second squared, m/s2: units for measuring acceleration

Notations

Abbreviation Meaning
u Initial velocity
v Final velocity
a Acceleration
t Time
s Distance
